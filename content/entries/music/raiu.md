# raiu

a song about thunderstorms (raiu - 雷雨)

"The rain to the wind said,
'You push and I'll pelt.'
They so smote the garden bed
That the flowers actually knelt,
And lay lodged -- though not dead.
I know how the flowers felt."
― Robert Frost
